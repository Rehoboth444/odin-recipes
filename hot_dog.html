Hot dog

Home

This hot dog features a toasted bun that is golden brown and crisp, holding a grilled sausage with shallow diagonal cuts across its surface for extra texture and presentation.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Sausages
  • Butter or oil
  • Herbs
  • Salt or spices

    • Steps

  1. Prepare the Sausages: Make shallow cuts on the sausages for even cooking.
  2. Grill the Sausages: Cook them on a grill or pan until browned and juicy.
  3. Toast the Buns: Lightly toast the hot dog buns until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the Hot Dogs: Place each sausage in a toasted bun.
  5. Add Garnish (Optional): Decorate with fresh herbs if desired, and serve hot.
