The two loaves of sliced bread neatly arranged in glass trays. The bread in the front is topped with sesame seeds, giving it a golden and textured appearance, while the loaf in the back appears plain with a smooth, shiny crust.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeastl
  • Salt
  • Sesame seeds

    • Steps

  1. Make the Dough: Mix flour, water, yeast, and salt into a smooth dough.
  2. Let It Rise: Cover the dough and let it rise until it doubles in size.
  3. Shape the Loaves: Divide the dough, shape it into loaves, and place them in baking trays.
  4. Add Toppings: Sprinkle sesame seeds on top if desired.
  5. Bake the Bread: Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) until golden brown. Let it cool and slice.
