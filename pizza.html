A freshly baked margherita pizza served on a black pizza pan. The pizza features a golden-brown, crispy crust with a slightly blistered texture. It is topped with a simple yet appetizing layer of melted cheese and streaks of vibrant red tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

    • Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: Roll out your pizza dough into a round shape and place it on a baking pan.
  2. Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Add Cheese: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering it generously.
  4. Add Toppings: Scatter fresh basil leaves on top and drizzle a little olive oil.
  5. Bake the Pizza: Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
